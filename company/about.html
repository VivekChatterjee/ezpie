About Ezpie

Learn programming, play challenges and write blog posts

In Ezpie you can do a lot of things, from reading a blog post to writing one yourself, but the most greatest thing of all is doing challenges! In ezpie you can do challenges with other learners once in every week! And get results ever next week with rankings!

Ezpie's Mission

Help new developers get the hang of programming

Ezpie is made to help you learn programming by reading, sharing and playing challenges. Here at ezpie the simplest example is considered the best example.

Ezpie's upcomming plans

Make ezpie reach ever developer

For the future ezpie have plans of creating an vscode extension to help vscode users to easily search for blog posts, create easy chrome extension for the same and a lot more!
